**Strategic Plan for Building Horizons Architecture's Tech Dream Team with Optimized Costs**

As an expert in tech talent acquisition and financing, I understand the challenges of assembling a high-performing team without overextending resources. Below is a comprehensive strategy tailored for Horizons Architecture (HA) to build your tech dream team efficiently and sustainably.

---

### **1. Define the Optimal Team Structure**

**Core In-House Team (Essential Roles):**

- **CTO (Oscar):** Continue leading technology strategy and innovation.
- **Lead Software Architect:** Responsible for system architecture, ensuring scalability and robustness.
- **Senior Full-Stack Developer:** Versatile in both frontend and backend to accelerate development.
- **Product Manager:** Aligns tech projects with business objectives and manages the product lifecycle.
- **QA/Test Engineer:** Maintains product quality through rigorous testing protocols.

**Supplementary Team (Flexible Roles):**

- **Freelancers/Consultants:**
    - **Specialized Developers:** For specific technologies or features (e.g., mobile apps, AI integration).
    - **UI/UX Designers:** Collaborate with ANAGRAMA to ensure consistent design.
    - **DevOps Engineer:** Optimize deployment pipelines and infrastructure (as needed).

### **2. Leverage University Partnerships for Talent Acquisition**

**Advantages:**

- **Cost-Effective Talent:** Access to enthusiastic students and recent graduates at lower salary ranges.
- **Fresh Perspectives:** Infusion of new ideas and cutting-edge academic knowledge.
- **Long-Term Investment:** Potential to nurture and retain top talent.

**Action Steps:**

- **Internship Programs:**
    
    - **Structured Internships:** Offer internships to students from tec-monterrey and UERRE.
    - **Mentorship Opportunities:** Pair interns with senior team members for guidance.
- **Cooperative Education (Co-op):**
    
    - **Academic Partnerships:** Formalize co-op programs allowing students to alternate between academic terms and working at HA.
    - **Project Credits:** Collaborate with universities to offer academic credits for work on HA projects.
- **Capstone Projects:**
    
    - **Sponsorship:** Sponsor final-year projects that align with HA's goals.
    - **Talent Identification:** Use these projects to identify and recruit high-performing students.
- **Guest Lectures and Workshops:**
    
    - **Visibility:** Increase HA's presence on campus by offering lectures on industry trends.
    - **Engagement:** Host hackathons or coding competitions to engage with potential talent.

### **3. Optimize Compensation Packages**

**Competitive yet Sustainable Strategies:**

- **Benchmark Salaries:**
    
    - **Market Research:** Analyze local salary trends to offer fair compensation.
    - **Tiered Salary Structure:** Create levels (Junior, Mid, Senior) with corresponding salary bands.
- **Equity and Incentives:**
    
    - **Performance Bonuses:** Offer bonuses tied to project milestones or company performance.
    - **Stock Options:** Provide stock options with vesting periods to encourage long-term commitment.
- **Non-Monetary Benefits:**
    
    - **Flexible Work Arrangements:** Offer remote work options and flexible hours.
    - **Professional Development:**
        - **Training Budget:** Allocate funds for courses, certifications, and conferences.
        - **Career Pathing:** Outline clear advancement opportunities within HA.
- **Company Culture:**
    
    - **Innovative Environment:** Foster a culture that encourages creativity and ownership.
    - **Recognition Programs:** Implement employee appreciation initiatives.

---

### **4. Strategic Use of Freelancers and Consultants**

**Benefits:**

- **Cost Efficiency:** Pay for services only when needed.
- **Specialized Skills:** Access experts for niche projects without long-term commitments.
- **Scalability:** Easily scale the workforce up or down based on project demands.

**Action Steps:**

- **Define Clear Project Scopes:**
    
    - **Detailed Contracts:** Outline deliverables, timelines, and payment terms.
    - **Milestone Payments:** Link payments to the completion of specific milestones.
- **Integration with In-House Team:**
    
    - **Communication Tools:** Use collaboration platforms (e.g., Slack, Asana) for seamless interaction.
    - **Regular Meetings:** Schedule check-ins to align on progress and expectations.
- **Quality Assurance:**
    
    - **Code Reviews:** Implement peer reviews to maintain code quality.
    - **Testing Protocols:** Establish testing requirements before acceptance.

### **6. Implement Efficient Project Management Practices**

**Maximize productivity while minimizing costs:**

- **Agile Methodologies:**
    
    - **Scrum Framework:** Use sprints to deliver incremental value and adapt to changes quickly.

    - **Open Source Solutions:** Leverage open-source technologies to reduce licensing costs.
