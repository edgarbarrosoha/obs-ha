
**2. Establishing the Foundation**

**Defining Governance within Horizons Architecture**

• **Fundamental Understanding**: Establish a foundational grasp of governance as it pertains to HA.
• **Adaptive Approach**: Highlight how hybrid intelligence and systems-thinking frameworks provide a structured yet flexible methodology.

**Key Elements of Governance**

Outline the essential components informed by HA’s six dimensions:

1. **Legacy**

• Defining purpose and long-term impact.

• Ensuring alignment with the institution’s mission and vision.

2. **Community**

• Stakeholder mapping and engagement.

• Building strong relationships with internal and external parties.

3. **Learning**

• Developing necessary skills and knowledge bases.

• Promoting a culture of continuous improvement and innovation.

4. **Technology**

• Selecting appropriate AI tools and digital infrastructures.

• Leveraging technology to enhance governance processes.

5. **Context**

• Adapting governance to external socio-economic conditions.

• Remaining responsive to changing environments and challenges.

6. **Projects**

• Implementing specific, aligned initiatives.

• Driving progress through targeted actions.
