Learning outcomes
Expresión oral y vocabulario
●​ Escenifica la transformación de un texto narrativo a uno dramático, realizando su representación teatral.
Lectura
●​ Compara y evalúa diversas formas de desarrollar un tema en distintos textos, así como los diferentes
recursos utilizados (redacción y gramática, sinónimos y pronombres).
●​ Identifica semejanzas y diferencias entre la forma de habla propia y la de otros hispanohablantes:
entonación, vocabulario, expresiones.
●​ Sigue el programa de lectura que mejora la comprensión, la fluidez y el pensamiento crítico.
Escritura
●​ Crea un texto dramático considerando todas sus características y elementos.
●​ Elabora una leyenda como producto de la transformación de un texto narrativo a uno dramático de su
interés.
Reflexión de la lengua, gramática y ortografía
●​ Reconoce las diversas voces narrativas (omnisciente, protagonista, testigo) en relación a los diferentes
textos narrativos de países latam.
●​ Analiza y aplica las diferentes categorías gramaticales (artículo, sustantivo, verbo, adjetivo, adverbio,
preposición y conjunción) en la producción de textos propios y leídos.

Subjects, Predicates, and Sentences
●​ Recognizes that every sentence needs a subject and a predicate together.
●​ Recognizes that a subject is the person, place, or thing that the sentence is about.
●​ Recognizes that a predicate is the part of the sentence that tells about the subject.
●​ Recognizes that the verb is part of the predicate and tells about the subject.
●​ Identifies the four types of sentences: declarative, interrogative, exclamatory, and imperative.
●​ Enhances the ability to speak about a variety of topics.
●​ Practices reading comprehension and writing composition as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
●​ Practices listening and verbal ability as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
Nouns
●​ Explains what nouns are.
●​ Identifies the different types of nouns.
●​ Uses nouns effectively in sentence composition.
●​ Enhances the ability to speak about a variety of topics.
●​ Practices reading comprehension and writing composition as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
●​ Practices listening and verbal ability as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
Verbs

●​ Recognizes that a verb describes an observable action.
●​ Uses verbs to describe what the subject is doing.
●​ Recognizes that verbs are the main part of a sentence or phrase, telling a story about what is taking
place.
●​ Enhances the ability to speak about a variety of topics.
●​ Practices reading comprehension and writing composition as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
●​ Practices listening and verbal ability as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
Pronouns
●​ Uses pronouns as a way to identify or refer to someone.
●​ Recognizes that a pronoun replaces a noun.
●​ Distinguishes among the kinds of pronouns: personal, reflexive, reciprocal, relative, indefinite pronouns,
noun phrases (NPs), or determiner phrases (DPs).
●​ Enhances the ability to speak about a variety of topics.
●​ Practices reading comprehension and writing composition as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
●​ Practices listening and verbal ability as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
Adjectives and Adverbs
●​ Recognizes that adjectives give more specific information about an object's characteristics.
●​ Memorizes that an adjective is a word that describes a noun or pronoun.
●​ Recognizes that an adjective adds further information and specifies the meaning or nuance of what
someone intends to communicate.
●​ Enhances the ability to speak about a variety of topics.
●​ Practices reading comprehension and writing composition as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.
●​ Practices listening and verbal ability as preparation for the TOEFL iBT Test.

Science

Robotics

●​ Defines key concepts related to matter and change, including elements, compounds, mixtures, and
states of matter.
●​ Explains the structure of an atom, including the arrangement and significance of protons, neutrons, and
electrons.
●​ Uses the periodic table to determine the number of protons, neutrons, and electrons in various
elements, and model the electron configurations for selected atoms.
Manufacturing and Industrial Design:
●​ Designs and prints autonomous 3D objects using 3D modeling software and 3D printers.
Mechanics:
●​ Understands the principles of chain and gear transmissions.
Electronics:

Programming:
●​ Understands the GPIO pins and their functions on the Raspberry Pi.

Digital Planet

Digital Citizenship:
●​ Understands what AI is and how it is trained.
Fundamentals of Collaborative Robotics:
●​ Identifies and explains key concepts of collaborative robotics, including its advantages, limitations, and

Program Universal Robots:
●​ Performs basic operation tasks, including robot power-up/shutdown, manual movement, and
calibration.
●​ Develops robot programs using the Polyscope graphical user interface (GUI), incorporating motion

Lab for life

Self-knowledge and personal identity
●​ Explores and describes aspects of their personal identity, such as values, interests, and aspirations,
strengthening self-knowledge.
●​ Recognizes and values their qualities and personal achievements, demonstrating self-esteem and
self-confidence.
Attention and self-regulation

●​ Identifies that regulating attention requires awareness of thoughts and bodily sensations, and applies
mindfulness strategies.
●​ Uses self-criticism, humor, and self-love to address conflicts in a constructive way.
Decision making
●​ Critically analyzes information to make informed decisions regarding health, sexuality, addictions,
education, and participation.
●​ Recognizes destructive actions, emotions, or relationships and applies strategies to overcome them,
seeking help when needed.
P.E.

Capacidades coordinativas y físicas
●​ Conserva el equilibrio en actividades de mayor velocidad y con cambios bruscos, como giros, frenadas
o recepciones en movimiento.(Equilibrio)
●​ Se desplaza con eficacia en situaciones de juego, adaptando su movimiento a cambios de dirección,
velocidad y a la ubicación de compañeros y oponentes.(Espacio y tiempo)
Deportes handball
●​ Conoce reglas completas de handball, practica movimientos más complejos y entiende la estrategia
del juego.
Tecnología en el deporte
●​ Reconoce cómo la tecnología ha influido en el deporte y el rendimiento físico.

Music

●​ Creates and performs rhythmic melodies and group rhythmic improvisations, reading and analyzing
syncopated rhythms and basic chord progressions (T–S–D–T), including inversions.
●​ Develops instrumental skills by practicing chords and melodies on guitar and performing advanced
rhythmic patterns on drums, participating in instrumental ensembles.
●​ Demonstrates understanding of tempo and emotion, performing pieces with rubato, accelerando, and
ritardando, while engaging in active listening to analyze musical structure and emotional expression.

Art

Cinematography:
●​ Develops a compelling story, creating engaging characters, plot, and dialogue for a short film.
●​ Applies visual storytelling techniques, using camera angles, lighting, and composition to convey the
narrative.
●​ Directs and edits a short film, demonstrating expertise in pacing, timing, and visual flow.
●​ Collaborates with cast and crew, demonstrating effective communication.
