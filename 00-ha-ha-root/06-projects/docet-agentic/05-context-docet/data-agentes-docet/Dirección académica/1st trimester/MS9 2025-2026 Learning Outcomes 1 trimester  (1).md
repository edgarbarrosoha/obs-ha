English

Matemáticas

Reading Comprehension and Analysis:
●​ Critical Analysis: analyzes how writers use language, structure, and form to create meaning, and
explores themes, character development, and the impact of literary techniques.
●​ Follows the reading program that enhances comprehension, fluency and critical thinking.
Writing Skills:
●​ Argumentative and Persuasive Writing: is able to write coherent arguments, presents viewpoints clearly,
and supports them with evidence. This includes writing essays, reports, and letters.
Speaking and Listening:
●​ Listening and Responding: develops the ability to listen attentively to others, responds appropriately,
and builds on the ideas of others in discussions.
Literary Knowledge:
●​ Studies significant works of literature, classic novels, and contemporary texts to understand the
development of English literature over time.
●​ Considers the historical, social, and cultural contexts in which texts were written and how these
influence the themes and characters within the texts. Understanding Context.
Preparation for HSET:
●​ Continues developing the skills needed for success in High School English tests, including close
reading, essay writing, and analytical thinking. Building Exam Skills.
●​ Becomes familiar with the types of questions and tasks encountered in English exams, including how to
structure responses under timed conditions. Understanding Exam Formats.
Personal Development through English (Formative):
●​ Develops the ability to understand and empathize with different perspectives and experiences.
Encouraging Empathy and Perspective-Taking.
●​ Is encouraged to read widely for pleasure, developing a lifelong habit of reading and exploring
different genres and authors. Promoting a Love of Reading.

Lab for life

Identity and life project
●​ Deepens the exploration of personal identity by analyzing values, interests, and aspirations to
strengthen a personal life project.
●​ Critically examines the influence of others’ opinions and the media on self-image, desires, and value
systems.
Self-awareness
●​ Recognizes and manages emotions with greater awareness, expressing them respectfully and analyzing
the situations that trigger them.
●​ Applies mindfulness and sustained concentration techniques to regulate thoughts and sensations in
academic and personal contexts.
Resilience
●​ Interprets difficulties as opportunities for growth, facing them with composure and a long-term
perspective.
●​ Evaluates achievements and the effort invested in them, showing willingness to take on new challenges
that contribute to holistic development.
●​ Adjusts and redefines cognitive and emotional strategies to advance toward personal and collective
goals.
