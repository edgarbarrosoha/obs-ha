School year 2025-2026
Instituto Docet

2nd Trimester learning outcomes

Level: Middle School

Grade: 5th
2nd Trimester

Subject

Learning outcomes

English

Reading
●​ Follows the reading program that enhances comprehension, fluency, and critical thinking.
●​ Enhances vocabulary by learning new words, using context clues to determine meanings, and applying
them appropriately in different contexts.
●​ Improves skills in making logical inferences and predictions based on the information given.
Grammar
●​ Gains proficiency in identifying and correcting issues related to sentence structure, such as fragments,
run-ons, and parallelism.
●​ Focuses on using a range of vocabulary and correct grammar to convey ideas effectively.
Writing
●​ After reading a passage, writes a summary or paraphrase of the main points. This helps reinforce
understanding and retention.
●​ Practices presenting a clear thesis statement and supporting it with well-organized arguments and
examples.
Speaking
●​ Enhances note-taking skills to capture essential information while listening.

Math

Factorization and fractions
●​ Understands the concept of factors, multiples, and the Least Common Multiple (LCM).
●​ Writes mixed fractions as improper fractions and vice versa.
●​ Understands, identifies, and writes equivalent fractions.
●​ Understands how to get a common denominator and obtains the simplest form of a fraction.
●​ Converts from a fraction to a decimal, and a mixed number to a decimal.

●​ Compares and orders fractions and decimals.
Adding and subtracting fractions
●​ Estimates, adds, and subtracts fractions and mixed numbers with like denominators.
●​ Estimates, adds, and subtracts fractions and mixed numbers with unlike denominators.
Adding and subtracting with decimals
●​ Adds and subtracts decimals with and without estimating.
Multiplying and dividing with fractions
●​ Multiplies fractions and mixed numbers with like and unlike denominators.
●​ Divides fractions and mixed numbers with like and unlike denominators.
●​ Uses exponents with powers of 10 to explain patterns in zeros and decimal placement.
Multiplying decimals
●​ Masters multiplication with decimals in any position (multiplicand, or multiplier).
Dividing with decimals
●​ Masters division with decimals in any position (dividend, divisor).
Español

Expresión oral:
●​ Amplía su vocabulario utilizando estrategias como el análisis de raíces, prefijos y sufijos.
●​ Argumenta y fundamenta puntos de vista de un tema elegido.
Lectura:
●​ Realiza lecturas sobre un tema de investigación y las comprende, resume y analiza para generar
opiniones y argumentos.
●​ Aplica estrategias de predicción, aclaración y resumen para mejorar la comprensión.
●​ Conoce las características y la función de las reseñas.
●​ Reflexiona sobre las diferencias entre los folletos y otros tipos de textos discontinuos.
●​ Sigue el programa de lectura que mejora la comprensión, la fluidez y el pensamiento crítico.
Escritura:
●​ Utiliza distintas técnicas como cuadros de doble entrada, mapas conceptuales y mapas mentales para
organizar, crear y resumir información.
●​ Analiza y crea anuncios considerando las frases publicitarias y su función, utilizando adjetivos, rimas,
metáforas o comparaciones, para hacer más atractivo el anuncio.
●​ Escribe documentos formales para hacer diversas gestiones.
●​ Redacta textos estructurados con introducción, desarrollo y conclusión, utilizando un lenguaje claro y
persuasivo y recursos expresivos para captar la atención del público y transmitir ideas de manera
efectiva.

Gramática y ortografía:
●​ Comprende las reglas y el uso de los diferentes dígrafos (ll, rr, qu, gu, ch).
●​ Identifica la función gramatical de la conjunción y la interjección en diferentes oraciones.
●​ Uso de palabras con C, S y Z.
●​ Realiza la división silábica y clasifica correctamente las palabras como agudas, graves, esdrújulas y
sobreesdrújulas.
Social Studies

●​ Identifies major geographic features of ancient China and understands how these features, including
natural barriers, influenced the cultural, political, and economic development of the civilization.
●​ Studies the diverse geographical regions of Mesoamerican civilizations and how societies adapted to
different environments.
●​ Describes key events, major dynasties, influential figures, and the development of laws, government
structures, and leadership in ancient China, including the role of emperors and the Mandate of Heaven.
●​ Compares and contrasts the political structures of the Olmec, Maya, and Aztec civilizations.
●​ Understands the basics of ancient Chinese economic systems, including agriculture, trade, technology,
labor, innovation, and identifies the main goods and services produced and traded.
●​ Studies the economic development of the Olmec, Maya, and Aztec civilizations, including trade
networks, agriculture, and technological achievements.
●​ Recognizes the cultural, technological, and social advancements of ancient China.
●​ Studies the historical achievements of Mesoamerican civilizations in writing systems, mathematics, and
urban planning.

Historia

●​ Ubica la Reforma y la República Restaurada aplicando los términos década y siglo, y localiza las zonas
de influencia de los liberales y conservadores.
●​ Explica el pensamiento de liberales y conservadores, y sus consecuencias en la política y economía del
país.
●​ Reconoce la soberanía y la democracia como valores de la herencia liberal, reflexionando además sobre
la importancia de otorgar el derecho de la mujer al voto.

Science

Earth and space science:
●​ Analyzes and classifies Earth materials by investigating the formation of sedimentary rocks, explaining
how fossils provide evidence of past life, and sequencing events that have shaped the Earth's crust over
time.
●​ Constructs physical models to demonstrate how weathering, erosion, and sedimentation change the
Earth's surface, and evaluate how different factors like water flow and wind affect these geological

processes.
●​ Designs and conducts different experiments in teams to represent the various steps of the water cycle
and explain their importance.
Digital Planet

Digital Citizenship:
●​ Analyzes fundamental AI concepts and their applications to evaluate their impact on society, including
both benefits and challenges.
Design and Animation:
●​ Understands the importance of user-centered design and explains the fundamental role of the user
throughout the entire functional design process, using SketchUp.
Keyboard Typing Skills:
●​ Develops keyboard typing skills focused on design and programming.
Programming Fundamentals:
●​ Learns to assign values to existing variables and uses them in loops in Python, using code org.
Online research and digital tools:
●​ Conceptualizes, designs, and produces a personal digital portfolio in Canva by curating a selection of
their work to demonstrate visual communication skills, critical thinking, and professional growth.
●​ Edits a video with the purpose of creating professional project presentations; learning to import
footage, trim clips, and arrange content for a coherent narrative.

Robotics

Fabrication and Design: (based on design methodologies)
●​ Designs complex 3D models and mechanisms, such as gears and transmission systems, applying best
practices to ensure precision, attention to detail, and printability.
Mechanics:
●​ Designs and builds automated simple machines and mechanisms using various materials and
components.
Programming: (phyton or arduino)
●​ Designs and develops interactive systems using digital and analog inputs and outputs, including
sensors and actuators like motors, LEDs, to create responsive systems that measure movement and
control devices.

P.E.

Capacidades coordinativas y físicas:
●​ Aplica mayor fuerza en juegos y ejercicios que requieren resistencia, como saltar varias veces seguidas
o sostener su propio cuerpo por más tiempo.(Fuerza)
●​ Mejora su velocidad en carreras cortas y en actividades que requieren cambios de dirección rápidos,

mostrando mayor agilidad.(Velocidad)
Beisbol:
●​ Conoce fundamentos básicos del béisbol (bateo, lanzamiento y atrapada), y comprende las reglas
elementales.
Senderismo:
●​ Selecciona adecuadamente el equipo y aplica normas básicas de seguridad en caminatas.
●​ Reads and performs rhythms in compound meters such as 6/8, 9/8, and 12/8, and demonstrates control
of tempo changes by performing rhythmic patterns at varying speeds.
●​ Recognizes and performs major and minor scales in Do, Sol, Re, Fa, and Si, both visually and aurally,
using melodic instruments and voice.
●​ Interprets musical pieces with expressive elements, applying crescendo, decrescendo, pp, ff, and varied
articulation techniques (slurs, staccato, accents) within small ensemble settings.

Music

Art

Art:
●​ Understands and applies the fundamental principles of photography, including balance, composition,
light, and the rule of thirds.
●​ Explores the concept of slow living and its relationship to art and visual culture.
World Art (Photographic beginnings)
●​ Learn about the origins of photography and its early processes.
●​ Understands the basics of film photography, including one-shots, film color, and developing, resulting in
photographic projects using a disposable camera.Reflects on photography as a tool for observation,
appreciation, and mindful living.

Lab for Life

Personal care
●​ Understands the relationship between self-love and self-care, practicing habits that strengthen their
physical and emotional well-being.
●​ Analyzes the personal resources they have to improve their self-care and prevent health risks.
Growth mindset
●​ Reflects on their mistakes, acknowledges their effort, and transforms difficulties into learning
opportunities with an optimistic and perseverant attitude.
●​ Identifies skills or knowledge they want to develop and designs specific actions to achieve them.
Gratitude
●​ Values growth opportunities and recognizes the importance of gratitude in their daily lives.
●​ Practices gratitude as a virtue that strengthens social bonds, recognizing how it benefits groups.

Theater

●​ Understands theatre conventions and basic techniques and uses them for creation and performance.
●​ Understands improvisational techniques and is able to engage in improv sketches.
●​ Recognises the unique features of the characters of commedia dell'arte, their context, and is able to
engage in the archetypal characterisation.
