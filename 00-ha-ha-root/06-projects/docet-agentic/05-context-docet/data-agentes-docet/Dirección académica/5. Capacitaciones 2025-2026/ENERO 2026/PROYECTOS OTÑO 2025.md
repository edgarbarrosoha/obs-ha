## Kinder 3 A - Nature in my school {#slide-12}

Objective: To give students a meaningful and engaging way to learn about the nature of our school environment, students will explore the plants, trees, animals, and natural elements that surround them every day. Through observation, inquiry, and hands-on experiences, students will develop curiosity and appreciation for the natural world, learn to care for living things, and discover how nature plays an important role in our school community.

Link documentación:

https://www.notion.so/docet/K3A-Nature-in-my-school-27f45fa0da0d802a835bdcc36edd068f

![](ppt/media/image23.png "Google Shape;141;p24")

## Kinder 3 B - Rocky Explorers {#slide-13}

Objective:  The objective of this project is to spark curiosity and deepen the understanding of rocks and minerals by encouraging them to explore and investigate the natural world around them. Through hands-on experiences, observation, and guided discovery, students will learn to identify and compare different types of rocks by their shapes, sizes, colors, and textures. They will also explore the ways humans use rocks in daily life, making connections between nature and their environment. In addition, students will be introduced to minerals, gaining an appreciation for their unique properties, uses, and beauty. This project aims to promote scientific thinking, language development, and creativity while nurturing a sense of wonder about Earth's natural resources.

Link documentación:

https://www.notion.so/docet/K3B-Rocky-Explorers-27f45fa0da0d8044812be3fe261bdb85

## Kinder 3 C - Movies {#slide-14}

Objective:  To help students understand the complete process of movie creation in an age-appropriate and meaningful way. To promote creativity, imagination, and curiosity through exploration of filmmaking elements. To encourage teamwork, communication, and collaborative problem-solving. To develop language, math, and artistic skills through project-based learning. To foster confidence and pride as students transform their ideas into a finished short film.
