## Who We Are

**Horizons Architecture (HA)** is a Mexican AI-native science and technology-based company that helps organizations navigate the complexity of their strategic challenges through a hybrid model integrating systems thinking, advanced artificial intelligence, and transdisciplinary innovation.

Born as a spinout from Tecnológico de Monterrey, HA combines research, strategic consulting, and technology implementation to turn data into decisions, projects into legacies, and human-machine collaboration into collective intelligence.

### International Organizations

#### Universal Postal Union (UPU) – AI Agent System for World Congress
| Field | Information |
|-------|-------------|
| **Client** | Universal Postal Union (UN agency, Bern, Switzerland) |
| **Period** | 2025 |
| **Event** | 28th Universal Postal Congress, Dubai |
| **Reach** | 192 member countries |

AI agent system for the World Postal Congress. The **2IPD Agent** connects to UPU knowledge bases and the United Data Platform to analyze indicators from the *Integrated Index for Postal Development* and respond to data-governed queries. The **Congress Agent** interacts with the 2IPD Agent in a dual dialogue format to contrast diagnosis and strategic guidance, aligned with the *UPU Strategy 2026–2029* and the *Dubai Business Plan*. The system was presented in three modes: voice, chat, and structured dialogue, demonstrating AI's potential to strengthen the analytical capacity of member countries in the global postal system.

#### LAC Dialogue – Global Health Architecture Reform
| Field | Information |
|-------|-------------|
| **Client** | Tec de Monterrey / Academic Consortium (funded by Wellcome Trust) |
| **Period** | 2025 |
| **Reach** | 120+ key actors, 60 interviews, 4 subregions |

Design of the hybrid intelligence architecture (human-AI) supporting the Latin America and Caribbean Dialogue on global health architecture reforms. | Field | Information |
|-------|-------------|
| **Client** | Tecnológico de Monterrey |
| **Period** | 2024–2025 |

AI agent designed to connect communities, projects, and startups in the *Innovation District – Expedition*.
