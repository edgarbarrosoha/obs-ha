## The Model: Discipline + Platform as a Service

Design Thinking never built the platform layer. IDEO stayed in consulting. Stanford stayed in education.

**HA is building both—plus platform as a service:**

1. **HA as discipline:** The book, papers, courses. What establishes HA as legitimate knowledge.

2. **The tools:** Mara (individuals), SIC-Q (governments), postal tools (UPU). Where people use HA.

3. **The practitioners:** Certified people who teach and apply HA globally.

4. **HA as platform:** Third parties developing specialized AI tools for specific dimensions.

5. **Strategic partners:** UPU, Tec de Monterrey, others—distribution nodes that take HA where we alone could never reach.

**UPU can be the partner that defines how HA applies to international organizations and public services.**
