## 3. What they are doing

We have a spinoff at Tec de Monterrey called Horizons Architecture. They're building state-wide government programs and collective intelligence systems, and working with international organizations like the Universal Postal Union—a UN agency in Bern—where they're developing data infrastructure alongside AWS. The UPU's Chief Economist called them 'one of the most promising agentic AI startups in the world.' Their focus is building the knowledge to make AI genuinely useful for organizations and people.
