# Expertise: Tec Institutional Voice

## Domain
Communication style and voice for Tec de Monterrey documents.

## Tec's Core Identity

### Founding Vision (Don Eugenio Garza Sada)
> "La educación lo puede todo" — Education can do everything

This phrase anchors all institutional communication. It represents:
- Optimism about human potential
- Belief in transformation through learning
- Responsibility to society

### Institutional Values
- **Innovación** — Always pushing boundaries
- **Excelencia** — World-class standards
- **Sentido humano** — Education serves people
- **Liderazgo** — Developing leaders for Mexico and world

## Voice Characteristics

### Tone
| Characteristic | Description | Example |
|----------------|-------------|---------|
| Aspirational | Reaching for greatness | "Not just preparing graduates, but shaping leaders" |
| Confident | Without arrogance | "We know what works because we've proven it" |
| Inclusive | Inviting participation | "Together, we can..." |
| Grounded | Evidence-based | "Our 80 years of experience show..." |

### Avoid
- Corporate jargon
- Empty superlatives
- Defensive language
- Comparison that diminishes others

### Embrace
- Concrete examples
- Student/faculty stories
- Data when meaningful
- Don Eugenio references (sparingly, powerfully)

## Language Patterns

### Spanish Institutional Style
- Formal but warm
- "Usted" register for official documents
- Technical terms in English when standard (e.g., "campus", "workshop")
- Avoid anglicisms where Spanish equivalents exist

### Key Phrases
| Phrase | Usage |
|--------|-------|
| "Rumbo al 2030" | Future orientation |
| "Liderazgo con sentido humano" | Leadership definition |
| "Educación que transforma" | Impact focus |
| "Conectando con el mundo" | Global perspective |

## Document Types

### Strategic Narratives
- 3,000-10,000 words
- Executive summary required
- Section structure with clear headings
- Evidence in appendix or footnotes

### Presentations
- Clean, minimal slides
- Tec brand colors (blue, white, accents)
- One idea per slide
- Speaker notes detailed

### One-Pagers
- Maximum 1 page (500-700 words)
- Bullet points for scanability
- Clear hierarchy
- Call to action

## When to Load This Expertise

- Writing any Tec-facing document
- Reviewing tone and voice
- Ensuring institutional alignment
- Adapting message for Tec audiences
