# **AI and Beyond for the Postal Sector: Exploring Generative AI, LLMs, and Diverse AI Technologies**

### Overview

This workshop, designed for the Universal Postal Union, introduces key AI concepts—including Generative AI, large language models (LLMs), and other paradigms—in a balanced format. It combines theoretical foundations with real-world case studies, hands-on exercises with generative AI models, and interactive discussion, showcasing influential models such as DeepSeek, Claude, and selected OpenAI models to address challenges in the postal and logistics domain.
### **Duration:** 6 hours (09:00–13:00 & 14:00–16:00)
### Date: TBD
### Objectives

- **Explore AI Fundamentals:** Provide an overview of core AI concepts, including Symbolic AI and Computer Vision, with emphasis on emerging techniques like generative models and LLMs.
- **Link Theory to Practice:** Illustrate relevant use cases (e.g., DeepSeek, OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) and discuss how AI can address challenges in postal logistics, customer service, and operational efficiency.
- **Encourage Practical Exploration:** Enable participants to engage directly with AI models, fostering an understanding of potential implementations within postal services.
- **Facilitate Sector-Specific Dialogue:** Encourage discussion on both technical and operational implications of integrating AI in postal systems.
### Target Audience
Personell, directors, and senior decision-makers at upu who seek to better understand AI’s fundamentals and its potential applications in improving postal logistics, customer communication, and strategical and operational workflows.
### Format and Methodology

- **Presentations:** Structured sessions introduce both broad AI concepts and diverse sector applications.
- **Interactive Demonstrations:** Hands-on exercises with AI models illustrate the practical aspects of the technology.
- **Individual and Group Exercises:** Practical individual and group work encourages participants to conceptualize AI-driven solutions tailored to postal challenges.
- **Open Discussions:** Time is set aside for Q&A and dialogue on integrating AI into postal operations.
### Workshop Proposed Agenda

#### **09:00 – 09:15 | Welcome & Introduction**

- Overview of workshop objectives and structure
- Introduction of participant backgrounds and expectations, with emphasis on postal sector roles and strategic challenges.
#### **09:15 – 09:45 | AI Fundamentals in Context**

- Brief introduction to AI paradigms: Symbolic AI, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and emerging generative models.
- Discussion on how these technologies support operational efficiency and drive strategic decision-making in postal services (e.g., sorting automation, routing optimization, and managing system complexity).
#### **09:45 – 10:15 | Generative AI & LLMs: Concepts and Implications**

- Introduction to generative models and large language models (LLMs).
- Exploration of key features, including their potential for automated customer service, document analysis, and dynamic query handling.
- Overview of how these models can be used to simulate scenarios, forecast trends, and tackle complex strategic challenges.
#### **10:15 – 10:30 | Coffee Break**

#### **10:30 – 11:00 | Use Cases and Applications in the Postal Sector**

- Presentation of practical examples (e.g., DeepSeek, Claude, and selected OpenAI models)
- Discussion on how AI-driven solutions can enhance tracking systems, streamline customer inquiries, improve route planning, and offer strategic insights by untangling complex data patterns
#### **11:00 – 11:30 | Individual Hands-on Experience: Interactive Model Exploration**

- Guided session where each participant interacts directly with a live generative AI model.
- Opportunity for individual experimentation with real-time outputs tailored for postal data analysis and strategic scenario simulation.
#### **11:30 – 12:30 | Collaborative Hands-on Group Exercise: Postal Strategy & Complexity**

- Extended group activity designed to develop an AI solution addressing a specific postal challenge—whether enhancing package sorting, refining customer interactions, or designing strategic responses to dynamic market conditions
- Facilitated brainstorming and collaborative problem-solving that integrates live model testing to explore both operational improvements and strategic approaches for managing system complexity
#### **12:30 – 13:00 | Q&A and Wrap-Up (Morning Session)**

- Open forum for questions and reflections, with targeted discussion on operational applications and strategic complexity in the postal sector
- Summary of key insights from the morning sessions
