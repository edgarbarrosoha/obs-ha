## 1. SERVICES AGREEMENT (Contract)

**SERVICES AGREEMENT**

**between**

the **Universal Postal Union**, an intergovernmental organization and specialized agency of the United Nations, represented by the Director General of its International Bureau and having its headquarters at Weltpoststrasse 4, 3015 Berne, Switzerland (hereinafter the “upu”);

**and**

**Antena Lans SC**, a company established under the laws of Mexico, represented by its Director, Ms. Gabriela German and having its office at Filósofos 204, Colonia Panorama, León GTO CP 37160, México (hereinafter the “Contractor”);

The upu and the Contractor may also be individually referred to as “Party”, or collectively as “Parties”.

**WHEREAS**

* The aim of the upu is, among others, to secure the organization and improvement of postal services and promote in this sphere the development of international collaboration and technical cooperation;
* The upu wishes to strengthen its Unified Data Platform (UDP) environment with actionable postal insights and advanced Artificial Intelligence capabilities;
* The upu has developed advanced agentic AI backend architectures and now requires professional user interface design, frontend development, and deployment services to make these tools accessible and intuitive for its member countries;
* The upu wishes to deploy a suite of interaction channels—including text, voice, and agentic dialogue modes—presented through a unified, high-quality web experience;
* The Contractor is willing and able to provide such specialized web design, frontend development, and deployment services in accordance with the provisions of the present Agreement and its Annexes;

In accordance with the above considerations, the Parties hereby mutually agree to this Services Agreement and its Annexes (hereinafter collectively the “Agreement”) as follows:

**1. Object and terms of reference**

1.1. The Contractor shall provide the design, frontend development, and technical deployment of the upu AI Agents suite and Landing Page, in accordance with the terms and conditions of the present Agreement and as further specified in Annex 2 (hereinafter collectively the “Services”).

1.2. The Contractor shall provide the upu with status updates every two weeks, or as otherwise requested by the upu, setting out the relevant services performed by the Contractor during that time frame, including any deliverables defined herein and the planned activities (next steps), in accordance with the specific provisions of Annex 2.

1.3. The upu shall remain responsible for the overall supervision of the Services, including, without limitation, management of the overall scope of the Services, approaches taken, definition and modification of schedules, completion procedures, delivery conditions and implementation estimates.
________________

**2. Entry into force and duration**

The present Agreement shall enter into force on its last date of signature by the Parties as indicated below and shall remain in effect until full completion of the Parties’ obligations as defined herein, and, in any event, for a maximum period of two (2) months.

**3. Remuneration and conditions of payment**

3.1. For due performance of the Services described herein, the Contractor shall be paid in one instalments of **CHF 5,750 (eleven thousand five hundred Swiss francs)**, in accordance with the modalities specified below, subject to full completion of the Services by the Contractor and acceptance of such Services by the upu in accordance with the conditions defined in the present Agreement.

3.2. Payments for the amounts specified in clause 3.1 above shall be invoiced in arrears on a monthly basis, and remitted within 30 (thirty) days of receipt of the Contractor’s invoice by the upu, subject to confirmation of acceptance by the upu of the Services delivered by the Contractor in accordance with the provisions of the present Agreement.

3.3. The amount indicated in clause 3.1 above shall cover any and all of the Contractor’s expenses to be incurred under the present Agreement, including any travel and associated subsistence expenses. No other fees shall be paid, with the exception of reimbursement of other unavoidable costs incurred for successful completion of the Services under the present Agreement, and subject to the prior written consent of the upu. Such reimbursement shall be subject to presentation by the Contractor to the upu of any and all documents constituting proof of such unavoidable costs.

3.4. The Contractor shall provide the upu with his banking details (including full name of bank, exact address of bank and account number) as well as any other information required for the remittance of payments by the upu to the Contractor. In this regard, all invoices shall be submitted by the Contractor to the upu at the following address:

Universal Postal Union
International Bureau
Finance Directorate

Switzerland

3.5. Each invoice shall make reference to the present Agreement.

**4. Rights and obligations of the Contractor**

4.1. The Contractor represents and warrants that it has the necessary experience, infrastructure and resources to provide the services envisaged under the present Agreement and in a manner consistent with the provisions of the present Agreement.

4.2. The rights and obligations of the Contractor shall be strictly limited to the terms and conditions of the present Agreement. Consequently, the Contractor shall not be able to claim any benefit, payment, indemnity, compensation or right which is not expressly stipulated in the present Agreement. In particular, any national tax which may be levied on the remuneration paid to the Contractor under the present Agreement shall not be reimbursed to the Contractor by the upu.

4.3. The Contractor shall perform its obligations under the present Agreement with due diligence and efficiency and in conformity with sound professional, administrative and financial practices. The Contractor shall equally comply with all laws, rules, regulations and orders applicable to the supply of the Services defined under this Agreement.

4.4. The Contractor shall act at all times so as to protect, and not be in conflict with, the interests of the upu, as far as those can be reasonably expected to be known to the Contractor.

4.5. The Contractor shall cooperate with the upu on the coordination of all Services to be carried out, and shall supply all information requested by the upu in the manner stipulated in the present Agreement, or as otherwise agreed upon between the Parties. In particular, the Contractor shall regularly inform the upu of the performance of the Services and, without prejudice to other provisions contained in the present Agreement, immediately inform the upu of any circumstance that may delay or impair the fulfilment of its contractual obligations. It shall also inform the upu of any developments that may result in modifications to the Services provided by the Contractor to the upu. In accordance with clause 3.3 above, no additional fees shall be paid by the upu to the Contractor for any such modifications.

4.6. Except as otherwise specified in the present Agreement, English shall be used by the Contractor in all written communications to the upu with respect to the performance of the obligations under the present Agreement and with respect to all documents procured or prepared by the Contractor pertaining to such obligations.

4.7. The location for performance of the Services under this Agreement shall be the premises of the Contractor in León, Guanajuato**, México and may also comprise other locations specifically designated by the upu for performance of the Services described herein.

**5. Rights and obligations of the upu**

5.1. The upu shall pay to the Contractor the amounts defined herein as per the terms and conditions set forth in clause 3 above.

5.2. At its sole discretion, the upu may provide the Contractor access to upu premises and management systems, including any documentation and technical support, adequate work space, translation, printing and distribution facilities, if deemed necessary for due performance of the Services by the Contractor under the present Agreement.

**6. Confidentiality**

In addition to the confidentiality provisions contained in Article 11 of Annex 1 and in line with general principles of transparency established by the United Nations, the upu shall be entitled to publicly disclose, discuss, communicate and report on the terms of the present Agreement to the relevant bodies of the upu, their members, the upu member countries and their designated operators, as deemed necessary by the upu.

**7. Default**

If a Party does not comply with the deadlines set out in the present Agreement, it shall be in default without further notice. In all other cases, a Party shall be in default only after being so notified and given a reasonable deadline within which to perform.

**8. Liability**

Without prejudice to the relevant provisions contained in Article 6 of Annex 1, the aggregate liability of a Party shall be limited to 10% (ten percent) of the total remuneration estimated for the present Agreement. This limitation of liability shall not apply to any liability of the Contractor arising from infringement of intellectual property rights. Nothing in this Agreement shall prevent or limit either Party's liability for any claims, demands, costs, losses and expenses whatsoever arising out of or in connection with fraud, intentional fault, willful misconduct and gross negligence.

Accordingly, the Contractor shall immediately contact the upu in case of any violation or attempt to violate the privileges and immunities of the upu as referred to herein, and take any necessary measures to prevent such violations.

10.3. Should any provision of the present Agreement be held invalid or prohibited, such provision shall be ineffective only to the extent of such invalidity or prohibition and shall not invalidate the remainder of such provision or the remaining provisions of the present Agreement.
