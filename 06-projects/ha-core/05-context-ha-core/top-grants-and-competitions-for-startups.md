## 1. Startup World Cup

**Prize:** $1 million investment  
**Details:** This global competition brings together regional winners from over 60 countries to compete for a $1 million investment prize[](https://www.startupworldcup.io/).

## 2. HICOOL Global Entrepreneurship Competition

**Prize:** Up to $281,000 per startup  
**Details:** China's largest startup competition with a total prize pool of $14 million. Offers multiple prize tiers, with the top prize being $281,000 per startup[](https://www.f6s.com/global-entrepreneurship-competition-2024).

## 3. Seedstars World Competition

**Prize:** Up to $500,000 in investment  
**Details:** A global startup competition focused on emerging markets, offering investments up to $500,000 and additional prizes[](https://grasshopper.com/blog/startup-competition-guide/).

## 4. Arch Grants Startup Competition

**Prize:** Up to $100,000 in equity-free funding  
**Details:** Offers $75,000 non-dilutive grants plus an additional $25,000 for companies relocating to St. Louis, Missouri[](https://archgrants.org/competition/).

## 5. 1ST50K Startup Competition

**Prize:** Up to $50,000 in cash grants  
**Details:** Offers up to $50,000 in non-dilutive funding plus pro-bono services for startups willing to locate in Cape Girardeau, Missouri[](https://www.codefiworks.com/1st50k/).

## 6. Venture Kick (Switzerland)

**Prize:** Up to CHF 150,000 (approximately $165,000 USD)  
**Details:** A Swiss initiative supporting university spin-offs with staged funding up to CHF 150,000[](https://fintechnews.ch/funding/top-grants-and-competitions-for-fintech-startups-in-switzerland/71855/).

## 7. >>venture>> Competition (Switzerland)

**Prize:** Up to CHF 150,000 (approximately $165,000 USD)  
**Details:** A Swiss startup competition offering prizes across multiple categories, with a grand prize of CHF 100,000[](https://fintechnews.ch/funding/top-grants-and-competitions-for-fintech-startups-in-switzerland/71855/).

## 8. EU-Startups Pitch Competition

**Prize:** Prize package worth €385,000 (approximately $420,000 USD)  
**Details:** Annual competition for European early-stage startups[](https://www.alphagamma.eu/entrepreneurship/startup-competitions-2024/).

## 9. Hello Tomorrow Global Challenge

**Prize:** €100,000 (approximately $109,000 USD) grand prize  
**Details:** Global competition for deep-tech startups addressing industrial, societal, or environmental challenges[](https://impactprosper.com/impact-pitch-competitions-europe/).These competitions offer significant funding opportunities for startups, ranging from $50,000 to $1 million, along with valuable networking, mentorship, and exposure. It's important to note that eligibility criteria, application deadlines, and specific prize details may vary, so startups should carefully review the requirements for each competition before applying.
