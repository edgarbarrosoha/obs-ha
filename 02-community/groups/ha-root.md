# HA ROOT

• Horizons Architecture (HA) is a spinout from Tecnológico de Monterrey focused on enhancing human-machine interactions to address and transform complex challenges.
• Develops a systemic framework combining AI, data systems, and a multi-user fractal network to facilitate strategic decision-making and collaboration.
• Its methodology transforms complexity into a competitive advantage by integrating systems thinking and artificial intelligence.
• The HA system is composed of three core elements: System Thinking Framework, AI and Data System, and Multi-User Fractal Network.
• HA’s approach documents and transforms organizational and collective efforts into insights that create value for both current and future human and AI generations.
