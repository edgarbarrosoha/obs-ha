# Gobierno GTO 2024-2030

• The Government of the State of Guanajuato is the public administration of the state, structured under three powers: Executive, Legislative, and Judicial, and composed of 46 autonomous municipalities.
• Its mission is to govern, plan, execute, and regulate the political and administrative activities of the state, based on democratic and republican principles that promote the common good.
• The Executive Power, led by the Governor, manages public administration through key departments such as the Secretariat of Government.
• The Legislative Power, represented by the State Congress, is responsible for creating laws and carrying out legislative functions.
• The Judicial Power ensures justice and legal certainty across the state.
• Main functions include governance, fiscal planning and budgeting, legal regulation, democratic strengthening, and public asset management.
