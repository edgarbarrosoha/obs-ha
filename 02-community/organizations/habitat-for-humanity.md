# HABITAT FOR HUMANITY

• Habitat for Humanity International is a global nonprofit organization focused on building safe, affordable housing and vibrant communities.
• Since 1976, has helped over 62 million people build their futures through housing access.
• Engages in home building, repair, financing, policy advocacy, innovation in construction, and community development.
• Operates with volunteers and promotes inclusive, sustainable solutions.
