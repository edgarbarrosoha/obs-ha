# Palacio de Hierro

• Mexican luxury department store chain founded in 1891, part of Grupo BAL.
• First department store in Mexico, inspired by European and American models, named after its original iron-and-steel structure.
• Known for its slogan “Soy Totalmente Palacio,” symbolizing luxury and exclusivity.
• Offers high-end fashion, accessories, beauty, jewelry, home décor, and lifestyle services.
• Operates specialized brands such as Casa Palacio (home design), Viajes Palacio (travel), and La Tarjeta Palacio (credit services).
