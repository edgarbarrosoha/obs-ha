# IECA

• Public decentralized institution providing vocational training to develop workforce skills and promote employment, self-employment, and economic development in Guanajuato.
• Offers courses in traditional trades and technical specializations, delivered both in-person and online.
• Supports individuals, businesses, educational institutions, government, and community sectors.
• Provides certifications recognized by the federal Ministry of Education (SEP) to enhance employability.
• Flexible learning options: at IECA facilities, company sites, community centers, or online via IECA Virtual.
