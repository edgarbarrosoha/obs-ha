# SEG

• The Secretaría de Educación de Guanajuato (SEG) is the state institution responsible for implementing educational policy in Guanajuato.
• Oversees preschool, basic, middle, and non-university higher education levels.
• Ensures access to quality education, promotes student retention, and prevents educational lag.
• Authorizes and supervises private institutions through the RVOE (Recognition of Official Study Validity).
• Provides educational statistics and analysis via the Integrated Educational Information System (SIIE).
• Develops programs such as Dual Education to strengthen collaboration between education and industry.
• Mission: Improve quality of life and social well-being through innovative, inclusive, and equitable educational services.
