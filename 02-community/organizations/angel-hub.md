# Angel Hub

• Angel Hub Ventures is a private investment club based in San Pedro, Nuevo León.
• It connects entrepreneurs with investors interested in diversifying their portfolios through early-stage startup investments.
• Focused on empowering Mexican and international startups by providing not only capital but also strategic support (“smart money”).
• Founded in 2020 to strengthen the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Mexico.
