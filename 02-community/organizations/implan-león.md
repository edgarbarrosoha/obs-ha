# IMPLAN León

• Decentralized public agency that advises and guides the municipality of León in long-term urban and territorial planning.
• Develops strategic plans and projects to promote sustainable development and improve quality of life.
• Formulates policies for equitable and organized city growth.
• Provides technical advice to the City Council and municipal departments.
• Coordinates with other agencies to ensure continuity of projects across administrations.
• Designs infrastructure projects and public policies, including mobility plans, parks, and roadways.
