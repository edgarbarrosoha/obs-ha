# Senado de la República

• The Senate of the Republic of Mexico is the upper house of the Congress of the Union and part of the Legislative Power, representing the federal states and working with the Chamber of Deputies to draft and approve laws.
• Composed of 128 senators elected every six years: 64 by relative majority, 32 by first minority, and 32 by proportional representation.
• Main functions include: legislation, federal representation, balance of powers, foreign policy approval, appointment of top officials (e.g., Supreme Court ministers, Attorney General), and national security oversight.
