# EGADE

• EGADE Business School is the graduate business school of Tecnológico de Monterrey, recognized as a top institution in Mexico and Latin America.
• Develops globally competitive leaders with an entrepreneurial spirit and human-centered values.
• Campuses in Mexico City, Monterrey, Guadalajara, and Querétaro.
• Ranked as the leading business school in Latin America by QS Global MBA & Business Master’s Rankings (2018–2024) and Eduniversal (2008–2023).
• Holds the prestigious “Triple Crown” accreditations (AACSB, AMBA, EQUIS), shared by only 0.15% of global business schools.
