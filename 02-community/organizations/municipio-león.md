# Municipio León

• León de los Aldama is a major Mexican city in Guanajuato and the largest urban area in the Bajío region, with over 1.6 million inhabitants.
• The León City Council (Ayuntamiento de León) manages municipal administration, urban services, and local development.
• Founded in 1576, it serves as the municipal seat and promotes León as a leading city in governance, infrastructure, and public services.
