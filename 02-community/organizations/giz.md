# GIZ

• The Deutsche Gesellschaft für Internationale Zusammenarbeit (GIZ) GmbH is a German federal enterprise providing services in international cooperation for sustainable development and global education.
• With over 50 years of experience, GIZ works in areas such as economic development, peace and security, energy, and environmental protection.
• It partners with the German Federal Government (mainly BMZ), the EU, the UN, private sector, and governments worldwide.
• Headquartered in Bonn and Eschborn, GIZ promotes effective, sustainable solutions to improve living conditions and support long-term global development.
