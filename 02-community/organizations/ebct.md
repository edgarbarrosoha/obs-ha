# EBCT

• The Technology Transfer and Science-Based Entrepreneurship Office of Tecnológico de Monterrey manages and protects institutional inventions and promotes innovation-driven entrepreneurship.
• Facilitates the creation of science- and technology-based startups through commercialization, licensing, and funding.
• Connects talent and projects with national and international innovation ecosystems.
• Secures national and international funds for scaling and accelerating innovation projects.
• Oversees intellectual property protection, valuation, and commercialization to maximize the social and economic impact of Tec’s research.
