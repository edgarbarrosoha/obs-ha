# IEEGL

• The Eugenio Garza Lagüera Institute of Entrepreneurship (IEEGL) is an initiative of Tecnológico de Monterrey that supports entrepreneurs in turning innovative ideas into real projects.
• Its mission is to help entrepreneurs and businesspeople develop and materialize innovative projects with social, technological, or business impact.
• It collaborates with Tec students, alumni, and external entrepreneurs to strengthen the entrepreneurial ecosystem.
• Offers guidance, training, and resources to transform ideas into initiatives that create economic, social, and environmental value.
