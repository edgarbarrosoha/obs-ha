# CEMEX

• Cemex is a global leader in the production and supply of cement, aggregates, ready-mix concrete, and sustainable urbanization solutions.
• Founded in 1906 and headquartered in Monterrey, Mexico, it operates in more than 50 countries with over 10,000 employees.
• The company focuses on innovation and sustainability to address global construction challenges and build a better future.
