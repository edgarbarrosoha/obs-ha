# EGyTP

• The School of Government and Public Transformation of Tecnológico de Monterrey forms leaders who positively impact public problem-solving through knowledge generation and innovative methodologies.
• Founded in 2003, it evolved from the Graduate School of Public Administration to a multidisciplinary institution focused on public transformation beyond government.
• Offers postgraduate programs in Public Administration, Public Policy, Law, Economics, and Political Analysis, with national recognition from CONACYT.
• Hosts a diverse faculty from top global universities with experience in public service and non-governmental work.
• Promotes transdisciplinary research, leadership for service, and technological innovation to generate public and social value in Latin America.
