# FEMSA

• FEMSA is a leading multinational company that creates economic and social value through its diverse business units.
• Operates across multiple sectors including retail (OXXO, OXXO Gas, Valora), health (pharmacies and related services), digital solutions (Spin by OXXO, OXXO Premia), beverages (Coca-Cola FEMSA), and logistics (FEMSA Strategic Businesses).
• Employs over 350,000 people across 18 countries.
• Recognized for its sustainability leadership as part of the Dow Jones MILA Pacific Alliance, FTSE4Good Emerging, and S&P/BMV Total Mexico ESG indices.
