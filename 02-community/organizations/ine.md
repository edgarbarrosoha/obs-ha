# INE

• The National Electoral Institute (INE) is an autonomous public organization responsible for organizing federal elections in Mexico, including the President, Senators, and Deputies.
• Coordinates local elections with state electoral bodies.
• Ensures free, fair, and reliable elections.
• Protects citizens’ political rights and promotes democratic culture.
• Manages the Federal Register of Voters.
• Oversees the allocation of state broadcast time to political actors.
