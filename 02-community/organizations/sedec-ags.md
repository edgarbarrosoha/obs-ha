# SEDEC AGS

The Secretaría de Desarrollo Económico, Ciencia y Tecnología (SEDECYT) of Aguascalientes aims to achieve sustained economic growth with a strong human focus to enhance the quality of life and competitiveness of local industries.
• Promotes sustainable, equitable, and collaborative economic development by ensuring a favorable environment for investment and business growth.
• Strengthens industrial competitiveness and leverages the state’s human capital to generate well-paid jobs.
• Vision: To be a national leader in sustainable economic development, quality of life, competitive and productive industries, world-class infrastructure, innovation-driven economy, and investment attraction.
