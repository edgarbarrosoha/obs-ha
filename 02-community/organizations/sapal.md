# SAPAL

• SAPAL (Sistema de Agua Potable y Alcantarillado de León) is an autonomous municipal agency that provides drinking water, sewerage, sanitation, and water reuse services in León, Guanajuato.
• Recognized nationally and internationally for its efficiency and sustainability.
• Promotes treated water reuse, self-generated energy, and citizen education programs for water conservation.
