# IPLANEG

• State-level agency responsible for coordinating and executing Guanajuato’s State Planning System.
• Develops and monitors the State Development Plan and the State Evaluation System.
• Manages the State Statistical and Geographic Information System (SEIEG) to provide reliable data for planning.
• Promotes social participation in planning and policy monitoring.
• Provides advisory and technical support to municipalities for their planning instruments.
• Coordinates with inter-state planning processes to foster regional sustainable development.
