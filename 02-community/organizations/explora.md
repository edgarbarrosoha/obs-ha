# EXPLORA

• Explora León is an interactive science center and park in León, Guanajuato, combining science, technology, culture, and recreation.
• Features thematic halls on physics, ecology, and technology, an IMAX 3D cinema, and green areas with a lake and recreational spaces.
• Includes “Explora Tu Potencial,” the “Libélula” environmental center, and the “Foro del Lago” for public events.
• Founded in 1994 to promote science and technology through hands-on, playful learning experiences.
