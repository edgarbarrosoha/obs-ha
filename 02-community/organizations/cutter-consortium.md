# Cutter Consortium

• Global research and consulting firm that helps organizations navigate digital disruption and leverage emerging technologies.
• Operates as an international collaborative network of over 150 practitioner-experts in business and technology.
• Offers consulting services, executive education, methodological guides, strategic diagnostics, and expert insights.
• Founded in 1986 by Karen Fine Coburn and headquartered in Arlington, Massachusetts.
• Publisher of Cutter Business Technology Journal, providing industry analysis and case studies.
• Acquired by Arthur D. Little, expanding its global reach and partnerships.
