# Eusebio Vargas

• Professor at Tecnológico de Monterrey, applying systems thinking, network science, AI, and data analytics to solve complex problems through holistic approaches.
• Over 10 years of academic and research experience in Network Science, Complex Systems, and Applied Data Science, with a focus on social, industrial, and organizational networks.
• Former Assistant Professor at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso (PUCV) and Postdoctoral Researcher at University of Konstanz, contributing to projects on network analysis and data modeling.
• Member of AI Mexico® and collaborator with international institutions on research integrating AI, graph databases, and machine learning for innovation and Industry 4.0.
• Holds a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics – Complex Networks Analysis from the University of Strathclyde and postdoctoral specialization from Universität Konstanz.
