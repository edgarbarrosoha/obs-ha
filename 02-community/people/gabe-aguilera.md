# Gabe Aguilera

• Academic leader with extensive experience in political science, international affairs, and higher education.
• Dean of the School of Social Sciences and Government at Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Monterrey, since 2019.
• Oversees programs in International Relations, Political Science, Law, Economics, and Public Policy.
• Holds a Ph.D. and M.A. in Political Science from Harvard University, and an MPIA in International Relations from UC San Diego.
• Former Associate Professor at the USAF Air War College and Assistant Professor at California State University, Chico, and ITAM.
