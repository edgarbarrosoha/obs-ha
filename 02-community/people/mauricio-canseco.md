# Mauricio Canseco

• Consultant in innovation and social inclusion with over 16 years of experience in social development, citizen participation, social entrepreneurship, and strategic philanthropy.
• Subsecretary of Social Investment and Strategic Alliances at the Secretariat of Equality and Inclusion of Nuevo León since 2022, managing public investment in civil society organizations and building strategic alliances at local, national, and international levels.
• Founder of Simple y Extraordinario and Mauricio Canseco y Asociados.
• Co-founder of Mindpedia and Movimiento Hecho con Capacidad ABP, focused on promoting dignity and competitiveness of people with intellectual disabilities.
• Studied International Relations at Universidad de Monterrey, Project Management at Universidad Tecnológica de México, and Impact Investment at Saïd Business School, University of Oxford.
• Ashoka Fellow since 2017 and DEI Program Leader for Rotary International, Zone 25A.
