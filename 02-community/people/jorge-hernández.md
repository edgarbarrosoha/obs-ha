# Jorge Hernández 

• CEO of Sinergia Global, an educational consulting firm focused on innovation, international trends, cutting-edge learning models, global mobility, and scholarship systems, working across public, private, and third sectors.
• Former Secretary of Education of Guanajuato, leading statewide education strategies and innovation policies.
• Founder and first Director of JuventudesGTO, integrating Educafin and the State Youth Institute to empower young people as key drivers of social and economic development.
• Served for over 13 years as Director General of Educafin, supporting youth empowerment through scholarships, loans, global mobility programs, and leadership networks.
• Background includes leadership of the Patronato para la Reincorporación Social por el Empleo del Estado de Guanajuato, promoting education and productivity in social reintegration programs.
• Holds an MBA from IEB and completed the Innovation and Leadership in Government program at Georgetown University McDonough School of Business.
• Recognized for building strong, high-performing teams and fostering impactful social and educational initiatives.
