# Lupita Valenzuela

• Subsecretary of Basic Education at the Secretaría de Educación de Guanajuato.
• Founder and Director of Indic2 Internacional, a consultancy focused on education and business development.
• Former Rector of the Universidad Virtual del Estado de Guanajuato (UVEG) (2019–2022).
• Has held various leadership positions within UVEG, including Academic Secretary and Secretary of Educational Access Centers and Outreach.
• Recognized as Woman of the Year 2023 by the Tecnológico de Monterrey in the “Career Achievement” category.
• TEDx speaker: Los colores de la infancia.
• Holds a Doctorate in Human Development (UNIVA) and a Master’s degree in Organizational Development (Universidad de Guanajuato).
