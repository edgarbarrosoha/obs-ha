# Pedro Moneo

• CEO and founder of Opinno, a global innovation consultancy connecting over 150,000 experts, investors, executives, and tech specialists in 20+ countries.
• Publisher of MIT Technology Review and Harvard Business Review in Spanish/Portuguese.
• Angel investor through Entrepreneur Capital, supporting high-tech startups.
• Former researcher in advanced nuclear reactors at Argonne National Laboratory; ex-consultant in corporate finance and strategy at Accenture.
• Recognized as Eisenhower Innovation Fellow, WEF Young Global Leader, and co-founder of XPrize Madrid chapter.
• Educational background in Mechanical Engineering (Polytechnic University of Madrid) and Nuclear Engineering (French Atomic Energy Commission).
