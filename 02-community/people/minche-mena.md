# Minche Mena

• Architect by profession and entrepreneur by vocation, with a multidisciplinary background in sustainable design, innovation, and education.
• Founder and partner of multiple ventures including NUUP Design, SHINE Architecture, and biodis, all focused on sustainable architecture, bio-design, and social innovation.
• Professor at Universidad del Rosario and Universidad de los Andes, promoting creativity, research, and environmental awareness in design education.
• Former Director of Architecture at Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus León.
• International lecturer and jury member for institutions such as the Architectural Association (UK) and University of Pennsylvania (USA).
• Committed to regenerative design, innovation, and active civic participation in urban development and competitiveness councils.
