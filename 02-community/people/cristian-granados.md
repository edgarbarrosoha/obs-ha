# Cristian Granados

• Innovation strategist, educator, and researcher specializing in corporate and frugal innovation, with over a decade of experience driving growth through research, consulting, and education.
• Academic Director of Tec Beyond at EGADE Business School, fostering leadership and innovation capabilities in executives.
• Experienced collaborator with startups, MBA students, and global corporations, helping organizations strengthen their intrapreneurial culture.
• Former Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Cambridge and guest professor at Cambridge Judge Business School.
• Author and co-founder of the EGADE Innovation Community, with research featured in MIT Technology Review and Stanford Social Innovation Review.
