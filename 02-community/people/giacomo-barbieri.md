# Giacomo Barbieri

• Assistant Professor at the University of Twente, focused on Human-Centric Asset Management and Smart Maintenance
• Mechanical Engineer with a background in Industrial Automation and over a decade of academic and research experience
• Former Associate Professor at Universidad de los Andes, leading courses on Maintenance Management and Digital Twins
• Active member of ISTE and IFAC (Manufacturing Plant Control TC 5.1)
• Research areas include Asset Management, Smart Maintenance, Cross-Functional Collaboration, and Decision-Making
• Holds The IAM Diploma and Certificate in Asset Management
