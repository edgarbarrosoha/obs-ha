# José Anson

• Lead Economist at Universal Postal Union (UPU), a United Nations specialized agency.
• Expert in artificial intelligence, blockchain, big data analytics, digital transformation, and postal/trade economics.
• Leads economic research, AI solutions, and big data analytics at UPU.
• Developed the Integrated Index for Postal Development (2IPD).
• Extensive field experience in Sub-Saharan Africa (missions to Niger, Benin, Malawi, Nigeria, Uganda, Senegal, Kenya, South Africa).
• Contributor to UN Sustainable Development Goals Indicators Framework and representative at UN Committee on Coordination for Statistical Activities.
• Founder of UPIDO, an innovation initiative.
• Previous experience: Trade Policy Expert at WTO, Research & Teaching Assistant at HEC Lausanne, Consultant at The World Bank.
• Holds a PhD and MSc in Economics from HEC Lausanne.
