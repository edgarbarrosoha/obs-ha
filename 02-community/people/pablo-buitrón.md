# Pablo Buitrón

• Research Professor at EGADE Business School, Tecnológico de Monterrey.
• Former Associate Dean for Executive Education, focused on designing innovative programs for professional and personal growth of executives.
• Over 15 years of experience in business education, leadership, and talent development.
• Former Professor and Director at IPADE Business School and Rector at Universidad Politécnica del Estado de Morelos.
• Holds a Ph.D. in Business Administration from Tecnológico de Monterrey and a Diploma from Harvard Business School.
