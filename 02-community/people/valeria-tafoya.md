# Valeria Tafoya

• AI and GovTech specialist focused on sustainable development, ethical AI, and digital public infrastructure.
• Council Member at World Economic Forum, Global Future Council on GovTech and Digital Public Infrastructure, shaping policy and technology strategies for governance and citizen engagement.
• Ethics and Technology Practitioner Fellow at Stanford, advising on responsible AI, AI labor, and ethical technology practices.
• Technology consultant and program officer at The Trust for the Americas, implementing AI tools for workforce reskilling and digital skills development in LAC, with projects recognized by UNESCO.
• Worked on AI policy and governance initiatives with PIT Policy Lab, including developing the Ethical AI Roadmap for Guanajuato, Mexico.
• Brief tenure as Head of Data at Endeavor México, focusing on data strategy and infrastructure.
