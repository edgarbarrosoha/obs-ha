# Luis Almanza

• Venture capitalist, innovation ecosystem builder, startup investor and mentor, and entrepreneurial educator.
• Director of Entrepreneurship at the Technology Park of Tec de Monterrey, coordinating the startup ecosystem in Chihuahua and leading programs like incubators, accelerators, bootcamps, and global immersion trips.
• Co-Founder of Orion Startups, an acceleration and investment program for early-stage LATAM startups, part of the Global Accelerator Network by Techstars.
• Extensive experience in venture capital, startup acceleration, ecosystem design, corporate innovation programs, and strategic policy development.
• Lecturer and blogger on entrepreneurship, innovation, and business strategies, contributing to multiple national forums and publications.
