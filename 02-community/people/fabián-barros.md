# Fabián Barros

• Creative Director, Designer, Digital Artist, Educator, and Consultant with over 20 years of experience in advertising, marketing, and digital arts.
• Integrates art, technology, AI, and virtual environments to drive innovation and digital transformation in creative industries.
• Founder of the La Reconquista art collective and the ZYX startup, focused on Metaverse experiences.
• Academic Coordinator and Chair of Digital Arts and New Technologies at Universidad ORT Uruguay, creator of Uruguay’s first BA in Design, Art, and Technology.
• Former Art Director at Ogilvy Uruguay & Italy and CCO at Grey Uruguay.
• Awarded Grand Effie, Gran Campana, and recognized at El Ojo de Iberoamérica, FIAP, and Lürzer’s Archive.
• Expertise in digital and interactive art, immersive experiences, AI integration, design strategy, and creative leadership.
