# Miguel Toro

• Executive Director of the Democracy and Development Initiative at Tecnológico de Monterrey, with 7+ years of leadership in academia and public policy education.
• Former Regional Dean and National Director of the Government and Public Transformation Program at Tec de Monterrey, leading cross-campus academic teams and partnerships with government, NGOs, and think tanks.
• Experienced Political Economist and Public Policy Analyst with international experience at The Wilson Center, CIDAC, and independent consulting roles.
• Specialist in Political Economy of Development, Public Policy, Political Risk, and Government Affairs in Mexico and Latin America.
• Holds a Master in Public Policy from the University of Chicago (Harris School of Public Policy) and a BA in Political Science from ITAM.
• Host of “La Plaza de Toro”, a platform for political and social dialogue.
