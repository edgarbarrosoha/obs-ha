# Alejandro Poiré

• Distinguished University Professor on Government and Public Transformation at Tecnológico de Monterrey, focusing on pluralism, democracy, and the future of governance.
• Former Vice President for Engagement and Development, Dean of the National School of Social Sciences and Government, and Dean at the School of Government and Public Transformation.
• Founder of México Crece, a project to enhance higher education quality and productivity in Mexico.
• Fellow at Stanford University; board member at Scotiabank Inverlat.
