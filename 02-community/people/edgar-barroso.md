# Edgar Barroso

• University Professor at Tecnológico de Monterrey’s School of Government and Public Transformation.
• Founder and CEO of Horizons Architecture, Antena Labs, Casa Antena, and Propositas — initiatives at the intersection of innovation, technology, art, and future studies.
• Holds a PhD in Music Theory and Composition from Harvard University and a Master’s in Digital Arts from Universitat Pompeu Fabra.
• Experienced in designing institutions, programs, and technologies for organizations committed to building the future.
• Focused on public entrepreneurship, hybrid intelligence, artificial intelligence, and innovation through creativity and collective imagination.
