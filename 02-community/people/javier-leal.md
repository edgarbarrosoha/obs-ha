# Javier Leal

• Urban Planner and Designer collaborating with mayors, developers, and organizations to enhance cities through strategic planning and design of complex urban projects.
• Founder of independent practice j-leal.com, working between NYC and Mexico.
• Former Head of Department and Head of Mobility Planning and Design at the Instituto Municipal de Planeación y Gestión Urbana (IMPLANG) in Monterrey.
• Past experience as Design and Planning Director at Capital Natural, Co-Founder and Design Director at Barco Estudio, and Urban Designer at Ciudad Emergente.
• Holds a Master’s Degree in City/Urban, Community and Regional Planning from MIT and a Bachelor’s in Architecture and Design from Tecnológico de Monterrey.
