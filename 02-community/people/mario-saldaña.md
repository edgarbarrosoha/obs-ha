# Mario Saldaña

• Innovation leader focused on creating value through innovation, fostering creativity, and collaboration.
• Director of Engagement and Business Attraction at Distrito de Innovación, Tecnológico de Monterrey.
• Associate Professor of Innovation and Entrepreneurship at EGADE Business School, Tecnológico de Monterrey, with nearly 10 years of teaching experience.
• Co-founder of Tru Innovation and previously co-founder of mindit, helping companies with digital transformation and innovation services.
• Former Innovation Manager at Axtel, responsible for Axtel Innovation HUB and innovation services.
• Background in Administration, Marketing (MBA) and Systems Engineering.
• Recognized for excellence in teaching and leadership in innovation.
