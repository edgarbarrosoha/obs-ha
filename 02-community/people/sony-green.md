# Sony Green

• Co-Founder and Chief Operating Officer at Kineviz, a company dedicated to helping clients “see clearer, decide faster, and engage better” through interactive data visualization.
• Over 15 years of experience leading business development, creative direction, and production for high-profile brands, campaigns, and live events.
• Previously worked in business development at Prellis Biologics, a tissue engineering company focused on bioprinting human organs.
• Former freelance consultant for startups and major companies like Salesforce, Yahoo, and IGN, producing creative content and strategic communications.
• Early career experience in the video game industry at LucasArts, where he served as Assistant Lead Test for Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic II.
• Holds a BFA in Sculpture from the Rhode Island School of Design and completed studies at Vancouver Film School.
