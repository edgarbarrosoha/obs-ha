# José Antonio Morfín

• President of the Board of Directors of the León Water and Sewerage System (SAPAL).
• Known for his active civic engagement in Guanajuato, serving as Secretary of Planning and Finance of the State of Guanajuato, Treasurer of the Board of Trustees of the León Foundation, Advisor to the State Electoral Institute, and Member of the Selection Commission of the State Anti-Corruption System and Governing Board.
• Recognized for leadership in public administration, financial planning, and civic governance.
