# Jose Anson

• Lead Economist at the Universal Postal Union (UN agency) with 20+ years of experience in economic research, trade policy, AI, and big data analytics.
• Developed the Integrated Index for Postal Development (2IPD) and represents the UPU at the UN Committee on Coordination for Statistical Activities.
• Expert in digital transformation, innovation, and AI for economic development, advocating for postal and trade modernization to drive social and economic growth.
• Conducted extensive fieldwork across Sub-Saharan Africa and contributed to the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Indicators Framework.
• Recognized for predicting the global economic and financial crisis and its impact on the postal sector.
• Founder of UPIDO, focused on innovation and postal development initiatives.
