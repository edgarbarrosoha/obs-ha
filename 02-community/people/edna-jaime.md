# Edna Jaime

• Political scientist with over 25 years of experience driving public policy transformations from organized civil society.
• Dean of the School of Social Sciences and Government at Tecnológico de Monterrey since 2023.
• Founder and former General Director of México Evalúa, a think tank focused on analysis and evaluation of public policies on public spending, security, justice, and anti-corruption.
• Former General Director of Centro de Investigación para el Desarrollo (CIDAC).
• Columnist for El Financiero and contributor to the radio program “Leonardo Curzio en Fórmula.”
• Holds a degree in Political Science from ITAM.
