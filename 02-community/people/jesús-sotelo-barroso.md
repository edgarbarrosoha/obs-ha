# Jesús Sotelo Barroso

• Senior consultant in Organizational and Business Development with over 25 years of experience in forming leaders and change agents across public and private sectors.
• Director at Gestión Integral Consultores S.C., leading consulting projects focused on organizational, business, and sustainable local development in Mexico and Central America.
• University professor and researcher at Universidad De La Salle Bajío, teaching graduate programs in Organizational Development, Business Administration, and Human Development.
• Former Senior Consultant for the Inter-American Institute for Cooperation on Agriculture (IICA) and FAO, coordinating regional development and innovation projects.
• Holds a PhD in Administration (Organizational Studies) from Universidad Autónoma de Querétaro and a Master’s in Organizational Development from Universidad de Guanajuato.
