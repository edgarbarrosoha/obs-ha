# Ricardo Godínez

• Innovation Manager at CEPLAS (Cluster of Excellence on Plant Sciences).
• Harvard-trained scientist and MIT-trained tech entrepreneur specializing in transforming breakthrough science into investable ventures.
• Focused on building deep-tech startup pipelines in plant sciences, synthetic biology, biomanufacturing, and AI/ML for drug discovery.
• International consultant for EU Africa RISE and Landell Mills, developing biopharma ecosystems and investment strategies in Africa.
• Co-founder of MIT Enterprise Forum México, fostering innovation ecosystems in LATAM.
• Holds a PhD in Organismic and Evolutionary Biology (Harvard University) and a background in Biomedical Research (UNAM).
