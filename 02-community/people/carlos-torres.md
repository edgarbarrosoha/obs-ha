# Carlos Torres

• Public innovation leader focused on designing, implementing, and influencing policy for local development.
• General Director of Innovation for the Municipal Government of León, leading public and social innovation projects.
• Former Director of Research and Programs at IMPLAN León, developing the Municipal Intelligence Hub.
• Consultant in public innovation at PuenTechLAB and member of the Global Shapers Community (World Economic Forum).
• Over 10 years of experience in public administration, urban innovation, and citizenship education.
• Former professor of “Citizenship and Democracy” at Tecnológico de Monterrey.
• Holds a Master’s in Public Administration and Public Policy from Tecnológico de Monterrey and a Bachelor’s in Regional Development from Universidad de Guanajuato.
