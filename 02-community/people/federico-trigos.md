# Federico Trigos

• Head of the Transdisciplinary Analysis of Value Chains and Global Opportunities research group
• Member of the National System of Researchers (Level II) and Mexican Academy of Sciences
• PhD in Industrial Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology
• Research on quantitative models for business performance: mathematical programming, industrial statistics, simulation, economic engineering, and corporate finance
