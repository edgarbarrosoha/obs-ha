# Marco Landeros

• CEO & Managing Partner at Anker Bioss, a global consulting firm focused on enhancing organizational readiness and strategic execution.
• Over 23 years at Nemak, serving in executive roles such as CHRO, Global Purchasing Strategy Director, and Managing Director South America.
• Former Chief Learning Experience Officer and Chief Innovation Officer at Talisis.
• Holds an MS in Management Science and Engineering from Stanford University and a BS in Mechanical Engineering & Management from Tecnológico de Monterrey.
• Fluent in Spanish, English, and Portuguese, with board advisory experience supporting leadership and transformation.
