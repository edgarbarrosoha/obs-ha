# Edmundo Molina

• Research and Faculty Director at Escuela de Gobierno y Transformación Pública, leading the strategic alignment of research initiatives with global policy challenges.
• Oversees faculty development, research funding, and interdisciplinary collaboration with institutions such as the World Bank and IDB.
• Research Professor and Director of the Decision Sciences Research Center at Tecnológico de Monterrey, advancing applied policy research in water and energy through data-driven decision tools.
• President of The Society for Decision Making Under Deep Uncertainty, providing strategic leadership and promoting global collaboration among academics and policymakers.
• Former Policy Researcher at RAND Corporation, with expertise in policy modeling, simulation, and uncertainty analysis.
